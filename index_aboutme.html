ABOUT ME

Katarzyna Żdan

Watch my story

As you already know, my name is Kasia and my adventure with makeup has been going on since 2014.
I started learning from the direction of "Visage and nail stylization", I came to the MAKEUPLACE Academy in Lublin and finished the 1st
and 2nd degree visage workshops. I achieve also the title of cosmetic services technician.
In addition, I took part in trainings with Daniel Sobieśniewski & Karolina Matraszek (qualified Make-up Masters)
and Maxineczka,at the renowned Pro Make Up Academy in Warsaw.
The summary of previous schools, workshops and training is passing the state exam and obtaining the title of journeyman in the profession of MAKE-UP STYLIST.


Visage is my passion and I am happy to devote time to expand my knowledge and skills.
I can confidently say that I am up to date with current makeup trends and novelties. I am constantly developing and undergoing training at such makeup giants as:

  • Ewa Gil,
  • Kamila Patina,
  • Aleksandra Kaj,
  • Aleksandra Kołodziejczyk,
  • Ewelina Zych,
  • ...

It all started with makeup and I offer it for generally speaking every occasion, I mean wedding, evening, photo sessions, prom...
In September 2017, I decided to go a step further and opened my beauty salon KEJT MALUJE.
In addition to make-up, I do facial treatments, waxing, henna and eyebrow adjustments, hybrid manicures and pedicures.
I am also pleased to say that I have recently joined my eyelash stylist, my sister Justyna.

If you like my work, keep your fingers crossed so that I can bring joy to as many people as possible in my KEJT MALUJE beauty salon.
Please feel welcome! :)
